ARSENAL FC

What is Arsenal FC?

Arsenal Football Club, commonly referred to as Arsenal, is a professional football club based in Islington, North London, England. Founded in 1886, the club is one of the oldest and most successful in English football history. Arsenal plays its home games at the Emirates Stadium, which has been their home since 2006, though they previously played at Highbury for over 90 years.

What is football/soccer?

Football (soccer) is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players. Players almost exclusively use their feet to propel a ball around a rectangular field called a pitch. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team by moving the ball beyond the goal line into a rectangular-framed goal defended by the opposing team. Traditionally, the game is played over two 45-minute halves, for a total match time of 90 minutes. With an estimated 250 million players active in over 200 countries and territories, it is the world's most popular sport.
